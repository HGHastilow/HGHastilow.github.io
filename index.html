Darcey Oliver Cake Couture is an award winning artisan cake company specialising in artistic showpiece cakes.

Sculpted, airbrushed, gravity defying, tiered towers, intricate sugar flowers or simply drop dead deliciously decadent, our cakes are commissioned for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, corporate events or any occasion that requires a show stopping cake!

The proof is in the pudding they say, and we believe that your cake has to be as delicious as it is visually stunning. Everything here is home-made, fresh to order using the finest ingredients we can get our hands on.

We are based in Wells, Somerset and offer consultations and tastings by appointment. We deliver throughout Somerset, Wiltshire, Dorset, Bath & Bristol and London.
