About me

Hi I'm Zoë, an award winning cake artist
and owner of Darcey Oliver Cake Couture.

I am a mad happy baker who has been creating in the kitchen since I was at my mamma's apron strings. My first novelty cake of a Jane Asher "lady in bath" design was made at the tender age of 13 (don't ask why, but it made sense at the time!), and I haven't looked back since.

In my former life I was a set decorator & props buyer, working on feature films and TV series. I got to do a lot of cool stuff like recreating Michigan in Surrey, dressing war torn London streets during the Blitz and dressing palaces for princesses. I've always enjoyed creating from a blank canvas and now I get to do it with cake (much tastier than film props...)

I fell in love with cake decorating on becoming a mother and children's cakes are still some of my
most favourite to design.

My cakes (and cake tutorials) have been featured in Cakes & Sugarcraft and the British Sugarcraft Guild magazines and as part of online global cake collaborations. You can also keep up to date with all my latest news and cakes on Facebook, Instagram & Pinterest.
