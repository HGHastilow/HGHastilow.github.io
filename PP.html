Portions and Pricing

All our cakes are bespoke and as such the price is calculated on size, complexity of decoration, quantity of modelling/sugar flowers, carving and sculpting requirements etc.

Finger portions are usually meant for fruit cake or heavy madeira sponge. These can be cut smaller at a dainty size and are usually served in addition to dessert. Dessert portions are as written and are generally cut for a more generous party slice.

The info below is meant as a GUIDE based on a fondant cake, with simple decoration. We have listed the most popular sizes but we can accommodate others too! For an accurate quote please send me a message.
